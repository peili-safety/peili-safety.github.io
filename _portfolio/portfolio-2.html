Intelligent Connected Infrastructure (ICI) refers to a network of physical and digital systems that are integrated through advanced technologies to enhance efficiency, connectivity, and sustainability in transportation sector. ICI leverages technologies like the Internet of Things (IoT), data analytics, artificial intelligence (AI), and cloud computing to create a cohesive and smart ecosystem.
